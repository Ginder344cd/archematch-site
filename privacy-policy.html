Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 14, 2026

Effective Date: October 7, 2025

Rainmaker ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Rainmaker platform at archematch.com and rainmakerapp.io. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

Important: By using Rainmaker, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and professional details
  • Profile Information: Job title, company, industry, and professional biography
  • Communication Data: Messages, notes, and correspondence within the platform
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed securely through third-party payment processors

1.2 Information from LinkedIn Integration

When you connect your LinkedIn account, we collect:

  • Basic Profile Data: Name, profile picture, headline, and email address
  • Professional Information: Current position, work history, and education
  • Network Data: Your connections and their publicly available information
  • Activity Data: Posts, comments, and engagement (with your explicit permission)

LinkedIn Data Usage: We use LinkedIn's official OAuth 2.0 API and comply with all LinkedIn Platform Terms. You can revoke access at any time through your LinkedIn account settings.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Features used, time spent, clicks, and navigation patterns
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session cookies, preference cookies, and analytics cookies

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Service Functionality

  • Analyze your social network to identify potential prospects
  • Generate psychological compatibility scores using Jungian personality frameworks
  • Provide AI-powered research on prospects using third-party services (Perplexity AI)
  • Create personalized outreach recommendations based on personality profiles
  • Identify warm introduction opportunities through mutual connections

2.2 Platform Improvement

  • Improve AI algorithms and psychological matching accuracy
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Develop new features based on user behavior and feedback
  • Conduct research and development on psychological intelligence systems

2.3 Communication

  • Send service updates, security alerts, and technical notices
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent, opt-out available)

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with trusted partners who assist in operating our service:

  • LinkedIn: For authentication and accessing your professional network data
  • Perplexity AI: For conducting professional research on prospects
  • Cloud Infrastructure: AWS, Google Cloud, or similar hosting providers
  • Payment Processors: Stripe or similar services for billing
  • Analytics Providers: For usage analysis and platform improvement

3.2 We Never Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and regular security audits to protect your information.

5. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Revoke LinkedIn access at any time
